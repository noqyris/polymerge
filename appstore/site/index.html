Polymerge

Merge matching shapes — every merge adds a side. Make the biggest polygon you can.

How to play

  • Swipe (or use the arrow keys) to slide all tiles.
  • Two identical polygons merge into the next one — two triangles make a square, two squares make a pentagon, and so on.
  • It's endless: the goal is simply to build the biggest polygon you can before the board fills up.
