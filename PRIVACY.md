# Privacy Policy — Polymerge

_Last updated: 2026-07-04_

Polymerge is a single-player, offline puzzle game. **It does not collect,
transmit, or share any personal data.**

## What the app stores

The app saves a small amount of information **only on your device**, using the
system's standard on-device storage:

- your best score,
- the biggest polygon you have made, and
- your sound/haptics preference.

This data never leaves your device, is not sent to any server, and is not
accessible to us or to any third party. Deleting the app removes it.

## What the app does not do

- No accounts, logins, or personal information.
- No analytics, advertising, or tracking (no tracking across apps or websites).
- No network requests — the game runs fully offline.
- No access to contacts, location, photos, camera, or microphone.

## Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not collect data from anyone,
including children.
